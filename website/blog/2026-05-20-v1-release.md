Campbooks v1 is now available. This first release includes the core features
that make Campbooks useful for managing your business paperwork.

## What's included

- **Email ingestion** — connect Zoho Mail, Google Workspace, or Microsoft 365 via OAuth
- **AI document classification** — automatic detection of invoices, contracts, receipts, and more
- **Review workflow** — approve, reject, and track document statuses
- **Full-text search** — find any document or email instantly
- **Email labels and tags** — organize your inbox with custom tags
- **Export** — push documents to Google Drive, Zoho WorkDrive, or Notion
- **AI chat (Scout)** — an AI assistant that understands your documents and emails
- **Multi-user** — invite team members and collaborate

## What's next

We're working on:

- IMAP support for standard email providers
- Mobile-responsive email reading
- Batch document operations
- Webhook integrations
- API for custom integrations
